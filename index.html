// About

Sound
without
limits

Based in London — the birthplace of drum & bass — this label operates at the intersection of technology and sound design. Five distinct artistic voices, one unifying obsession: pushing what electronic music can be.

Every release is crafted with meticulous attention to sonic detail. From the most delicate liquid DNB to the most abrasive industrial textures, the roster covers the full spectrum of the genre.
